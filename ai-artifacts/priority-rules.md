# Priority Rules

Critical:
- `lowestRating <= 3`
- Poor condition and ADT >= 25000
- Poor condition and scour risk
- Poor condition and fracture-critical risk

High:
- Poor condition
- `lowestRating === 4`
- Bridge age >= 70
- ADT >= 25000

Medium:
- Fair condition
- `lowestRating === 5`
- Bridge age >= 50
- ADT >= 10000

Low:
- No major risk indicators.
